# Title Softening Decision

## Recommended Title

*Production probability, transport, and grammatical possibility: Reanalysing the
English dative alternation with open data*

## Rationale

This title removes the premature neural-language-model promise and makes the
paper's actual spine explicit:

- production probability: the `languageR::dative` robustness result;
- transport: the BNC2014 generalization test now driving the project;
- grammatical possibility: the interpretive boundary the paper must clarify.

It also avoids implying that modern modelling has already produced a new
linguistic conclusion. The current result is that Bresnan et al.'s
verb-conditioning profile survives cleaner modelling. The title should point
past that floor toward transport and the production/possibility gap.
