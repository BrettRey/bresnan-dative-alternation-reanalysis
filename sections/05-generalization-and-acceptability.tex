\section{Generalization and acceptability}

This section will report the BNC2014 harmonization and transport result. The
first transport model shows that a spoken six-verb \texttt{languageR} model
predicts BNC2014 well above a marginal baseline, but remains weaker than a
BNC-native model. If acceptability resources enter later, they should mark a
level shift: production probability and acceptability preference are related
evidence streams, not the same measurement.
